Scrum Roles Information

Product Owner

"Keeper of the Vision"
Product owner prioritizes the product backlog and is authoritive! They add features to the product backlog as the product evolves. They make decisions about details when asked. They have the ability to accept/reject work at a sprint demo.. Key in the product backlog grooming meeting.

Scrum Master

"The Hall Monitor"
Lets people know if they are not participating or if their absent too much. Makes sure rituals occur. Encourages full participation in rituals.#1 PROBLEM SOLVER/Chaser when something gets blocked. Not a "lead/manager". Facilitator in all meetings, but most of all in daily scrum and retrospective.

Tech Lead

"First among equals"
The most experiences with tech and makes all the large technical decisions. Mentors other group members to bring them up to their skill.

Tech Chaser

"The Inventor"
Researches tech questions, very informal. Likes digging into topics and ideas. Finds training and shares it with the team. Likes to prototype and plan things.

QA

"The Doomsayer"
Asks questions and runs tests! Very useful if the dev team is not disciplined. Thinks of odd cases for devs to explore. Active when stories need to be fleshed out.
